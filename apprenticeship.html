Apprenticeship Programs at Peregrine T&C

Peregrine T&C's Apprenticeship Programs are designed for individuals eager to learn while earning. By combining hands-on training with structured learning, apprentices gain comprehensive skills in their chosen fields.

🌟 Benefits of Our Apprenticeship Programs

  • ✅ Earn While You Learn: Receive a stipend while acquiring valuable industry skills.
  • ✅ Structured Learning: Participate in a curriculum that balances practical work with theoretical knowledge.
  • ✅ Industry Recognition: Earn certifications that are recognized across various industries.
  • ✅ Career Advancement: Many apprentices transition into full-time roles within Peregrine T&C.

🚀 Available Apprenticeship Roles

📊 Digital Marketing Apprentice

Learn to create and implement online marketing campaigns.

🖥 IT Support Apprentice

Provide technical assistance and support for our internal systems.

📈 Sales Apprentice

Develop skills in sales strategies and customer relationship management.

📌 How to Apply

1️⃣ Application Submission

Fill out the online application and submit your resume.

2️⃣ Assessment

Participate in skills assessments and interviews.

3️⃣ Enrollment

Selected candidates will be enrolled in the apprenticeship program and assigned mentors.
