Orientation Session - April 16th, 2024

Are you a student at MES Mampad College—or an affiliated institution—eager to kickstart your industry experience? Join us for an orientation session to explore the internship program, learn about key opportunities, and understand the application process.

Key Internship Guidelines (FYUGP)

Essential Highlights:

  • Mandatory 2-Credit Internship: Complete during the first six semesters.
  • Minimum Engagement: At least 60 hours of practical experience.
  • Flexible Placement: Options include industry, research labs, or academic institutions.
  • Supervision Required: Oversight by an approved faculty or industry professional.
  • Documentation: Maintain a log book and submit a final report; placement must be pre-approved.

What to Expect from Us

  • Program Overview: Insights into the MES Internship Program.
  • Internship Opportunities: Explore various projects and roles.
  • Application Process: Step-by-step guide to registration and deadlines.
  • Interactive Q&A: Get your queries answered by MES and Peregrine T&C representatives.
